Our Mission

Our Vision:#

Grasping the Big Picture#

We break through the flood of information with analyses that are objective and fact-based from multiple perspectives. We believe that understanding and the pursuit of truth begin with critical questioning.

Our Mission#

In a global society where choices are often based on unfounded reasons and dominated by the status quo, there is an increasing danger that decisions are not critically questioned as they should be. When political and social discourse is increasingly driven by emotions rather than arguments, the autonomy of the individual suffers. This is exactly where “The Interdisciplinary” comes in. We reflect on complex topics from different disciplines and perspectives. Our goal is to replace the blind spot of emotional perception with well-founded objectivity. We want to provide the tools that lead to a nuanced opinion.

Our Values#

  • Critical Thinking as the Foundation: To question everything in general is a necessary step towards understanding the world around us.
  • Facts over Emotions: We work against the comfort of being guided by emotion and short-sighted narratives.
  • Multi-Perspectivity: We don’t view problems from an isolated point of view, but across disciplines to show the underlying connections.
  • Courage for Individual Thought: We encourage the intellectual freedom to question everything, identify potential mistakes and learn from them for the future.
